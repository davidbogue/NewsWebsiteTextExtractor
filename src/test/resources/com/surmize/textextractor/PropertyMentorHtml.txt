Wall Street faced its first decline in three sessions, as the market caught its breath after last week&#8217;s climb to multiyear highs. Nearly all the internet information providers finished lower with Google Inc. (NASDAQ:GOOG) and Zynga Inc. (NASDAQ:ZNGA) suffered noteworthy declines.</p>
<p><strong>Facebook Inc. (NASDAQ:FB)</strong> broke three-day winning streak as its hype looms over startups The stock’s price decreased in the last trading session with a previous 52-week high of $45.00. The stock was trading on below-average volume. The stock traded at a volume of 24.78 million shares at a price loss of -0.90%. The share price is now down -30.36% for the past three months. Latest closing price was 21.07% above its 50-day moving average and 26.19% above its 200-day moving average.</p>
<p><strong>Yahoo! Inc. (NASDAQ:YHOO)</strong> finished down after an alliance with Affle (a digital media company) for mobile advertising‎. While trading at volume lower than average, the stock fell -0.72% yesterday. Its previous 52-week high was $16.79 and moved up 5.96% over the same period, trading at a volume of 10.52 million. Shares have risen 4.28% over the trailing 6 months. The stock is currently trading -2.27% below its SMA 50 and -1.90% below its SMA 200.</p>
<p>In the energy sector, <strong>Alpha Natural Resources, Inc. (NYSE:ANR)</strong> continued to close up even after analysts at Dahlman Rose reduced the stock to hold from their buy rating. Over the last 12 months, a return on equity of -43.57 percent was realized due to the financial situation and earnings per share reached a value of -$13.36. Earnings are projected to move up 19.50 percent for the coming five years. The stock closed at $6.92, up 0.02 points or 0.29% from previous close and at a distance of 7.50% from 20-day simple moving average. </p>
<p><strong><br />
Vale SA (ADR) (NYSE:VALE) </strong>failed to maintain gains after rising over six percent on China measures. The stock moved down -15.01% in this year and its price history showed most recently beta was at 1.50. Overall the last 12 months, the percentage change in the price was -33.58%, hitting the highest of $27.60 on Sep 15, 2011 while lowest level in that period was $15.77 on Sep 04, 2012.</p>

		jQuery(this).children('li').children('a:first').addClass('selected'); // Add .selected class to first tab on load
	});
	jQuery('.inside > *').hide();
	jQuery('.inside > *:first-child').show();
	
	jQuery('.colabsTabs li a').click(function(evt){ // Init Click funtion on Tabs
	
		var clicked_tab_ref = jQuery(this).attr('href'); // Strore Href value
		
		jQuery(this).parent().parent().children('li').children('a').removeClass('selected'); //Remove selected from all tabs
		jQuery(this).addClass('selected');
		jQuery(this).parent().parent().parent().children('.inside').children('*').hide();
		
		jQuery('.inside ' + clicked_tab_ref).fadeIn(500);
		 
		 evt.preventDefault();
	
	})
})
